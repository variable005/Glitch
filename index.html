About The Event

Glitch 1.0 is a futuristic coding battleground where logic and creativity are tested, open to teams of 1-2 members.

Key Info

  • Format: 3-Hour Vibe Coding Session
  • Teams: 1-2 Members
  • Capacity: 50 Students Only
  • Venue: Amity University Patna

Participant Benefits

  • Gain real hackathon experience
  • Receive certificates for participation
  • Recognition for top teams
  • Boost your resume & portfolio
  • Network with peers & experts

Phase One – Idea Protocol

An online submission round where teams submit their project ideas. Only the top 50 most innovative concepts will be selected to advance.

Phase Two – Vibe Coding

The selected 50 teams compete in a high-stakes, 3-hour on-campus coding session, solving real-world problems under pressure.

Phase Three – The Mainframe

The best of the best face off in the grand finale with tougher problem statements and surprise twists for final recognition and glory.
