FAQs: Glitch 1.0

1. What is the team size limit?

Teams can have a minimum of 1 and a maximum of 2 members.

2. What are the key dates for the event?

The online submission deadline for Phase One is Tuesday, September 16 . Selected teams will be revealed later that day. The on-campus event (Phase Two) is on Wednesday, September 17. The date for Phase Three will be announced on the 19th.

3. Where will the on-campus event be held?

The on-campus phases will take place at Amity University Patna.

4. Is this event only for Amity University students?

Yes, this is an internal hackathon exclusively for students of Amity University Patna (AIIT department).

5. What if I don't have a team?

You can participate individually. We also encourage you to connect with others during the warm-up session to form a team.
